## Weather Update

- The summer warning has given way to a rain warning as the unpredictable climate in India has brought rains to many parts of India, bringing some much needed but ultimately disastrous relief from heat.
- While the showers are cool, and wet, and all; something nobody seems to be talking about is how insanely humid Bangalore has gotten. It has become nigh impossible to walk from point A to point B without becoming a sweaty mess.

> PS: these rains - according to convventional folk wisdom - are rather bad since the greatest driver of Indian Monsoon is the insane amount of heat generated in the hot summers of the Indian Subcontinent which draws in the moisture laden winds of the Arabian sea and the Indian ocean causing monsoon in India. If the landmass of India cools before monsson, the cycle can be broken.
