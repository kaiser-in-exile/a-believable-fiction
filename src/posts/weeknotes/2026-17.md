## Summer Warning

- The subcontinent of India has become a humid oven. Boxed in by the Himalayas, and baking under intense summer warm air has nowhere to escape, thus it seems is rather insistent on making life in this Subcontinent miserable for creatures of all kind.
- Temperatures in my hometown of Nagpur have already crossed levels which only existed in late May June, and it is barely end of April yet.
- I am really grateful I have been able to install Air Conditioning in the nick of time at my hometown, at least it is bearable there.

## The Unusual Heat of Bengaluru
- The city once famed for its garden weather seems to be experiencing an unusually harsh summer. Temperatures seem to flirt with the 40C mark every day and UV index scorches the ground.
- To be honest, this has been a while coming. Greenery seems to be the first casualty of metro construction in India, followed by the climate, and since Bengaluru Metro has picked up steam. I expect the weather to quite literally follow.
- I have succumbed to the heat, and bought a pedestal fan for relief. It has so far, by all accounts been a godsend. May god bless pedestal fans.
